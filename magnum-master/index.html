About Me

Hi! I am a senior Computer Science student at Biola University, with a Minor in Art. I have been drawing ever since I was young, though I took a long break in my teenage years. After being reinspired by Hollywood's most recent animated films, I picked up my sketchbook and pencil, and have been sketching ever since.

Most of my work displayed here is from my Figure Studies I class I took in Spring 2024. My work ranges from mediums such as graphite, conte, and charcoal.
